Our Story

FrootBeer came into existence thanks to a lifestyle change by its founder Thad.  Thad's realization of a gluten intolerance demanded that he seek another avenue to continue enjoying the art of brewing (and drinking) delicious craft beverages.  Today with his son Rob, they bring the creativity of craft beer brewing to the development of complex and delicious ciders.
